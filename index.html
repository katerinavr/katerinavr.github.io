About Me

I am a Research Scientist at Argonne National Laboratory, working on self-driving laboratories, autonomous agents, and AI-driven materials discovery. My research integrates machine learning, robotics, and computational chemistry to accelerate scientific discovery.

I have a PhD in Machine Learning in Chemistry from the University of Liverpool, and an MSc in Green Chemistry and Sustainable Industrial Technology from the University of York.
